c)	"Confidential Information" means any and all information of a confidential, proprietary, or sensitive nature disclosed or made available by the “Disclosing Party” to the “Receiving Party”, whether orally, visually, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, and whether before or after the Effective Date of this Agreement, including without limitation:
i.	Any information that is marked or stated as "confidential", or which a reasonable person would consider confidential in the context of its disclosure.
ii.	All information exchanged between the Parties in connection with any discussions or activities of a business nature, including proposed projects, negotiations, or commercial dealings, shall be treated as Confidential Information.

vii.	The existence, terms, and conditions of this Agreement and any discussions or negotiations regarding the Purpose, including the status or termination of those discussions.

iv. becomes available to the Receiving Party from a third party, provided that such third party is not under any legal or contractual obligation of confidentiality with respect to such information;

2. USE OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
The Receiving Party agrees that, during the term of this Agreement, it shall use the Confidential Information solely for the business purpose agreed between the Parties and only to the extent necessary to fulfill such purpose. The Receiving Party shall restrict disclosure of the Confidential Information to its Affiliates and Representatives strictly on a need-to-know basis, provided that such Affiliates and Representatives are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those contained in this Agreement. The Receiving Party shall ensure that all such individuals or entities comply fully with the terms of this Agreement.
3. OBLIGATIONS OF CONFIDENTIALITY
The Receiving Party acknowledges that any information related to the business relationship, including but not limited to the scope of work, deliverables, or implementation processes, is confidential in nature and shall not be disclosed or implied, directly or indirectly, to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party;
The Receiving Party further acknowledge that it shall;
a) Maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information using the same degree of care as it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than a reasonable standard of care;
b) Use the Confidential Information solely for the business purpose and not for any other purpose, commercial or otherwise;
c) Not disclose, publish, or otherwise reveal any Confidential Information to any third party, except to its employees, agents, or consultants who have a legitimate need to know such information solely for the Purpose, and only to the extent necessary;
d) Ensure that any such employees, agents, or consultants to whom Confidential Information is disclosed are informed of its confidential nature and are contractually bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those contained in this Agreement.
4. STANDARD OF CARE
The Receiving Party agrees to protect the confidentiality of the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information using the same degree of care it employs to safeguard its own confidential or proprietary information of a similar nature, but in no event less than a reasonable degree of care.
The Receiving Party acknowledges the sensitive and proprietary nature of the Confidential Information and agrees to take all reasonable steps, at its own cost, to prevent any unauthorized use, disclosure, or dissemination of such information by its Representatives. This includes, but is not limited to, ensuring that all Representatives are properly informed of and bound by confidentiality obligations that are no less restrictive than those set forth in this Agreement.
5. RETURN OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
a) Upon the written request of the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession or control, including, without limitation, all copies, summaries, analyses, compilations, studies, or other documents prepared by the Receiving Party or its Representatives that contain, reflect, or are derived from any Confidential Information.
b) Upon completion of such return or destruction, the Receiving Party shall provide a written certification to the Disclosing Party confirming that all such Confidential Information, including any materials containing or derived from it, has been returned or destroyed and is no longer retained in any form by the Receiving Party or its Representatives.
6. COMPELLED DISCLOSURE
If the Receiving Party is required by applicable law, regulation, or order of a court or other competent authority to disclose any Confidential Information, it may do so, provided that (to the extent legally permissible) it gives the Disclosing Party prior written notice of such requirement to allow the Disclosing Party a reasonable opportunity to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy. The Receiving Party shall cooperate, with the Disclosing Party, in any such efforts to limit or prevent the disclosure. Any disclosure made pursuant to this clause shall be limited strictly to the extent required by law.
7. NO LICENSE
Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as granting, by implication, estoppel, or otherwise, any license or right to the Receiving Party to use any Confidential Information, or any trademark, patent, copyright, trade secret, or other intellectual property right of the Disclosing Party. All Confidential Information shall remain the sole and exclusive property of the Disclosing Party, and no rights, title, or interest in or to such information is transferred or granted to the Receiving Party under this Agreement.
The Receiving Party further agrees that it shall not use the name, trademark, trade name, logo, domain name, design, or any other identifier of the Disclosing Party in any form of advertising, publicity, corporate communication, or product labeling—whether currently existing or developed in the future—without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.
8. INDEMNITY
The Receiving Party agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other Party, its Affiliates, and their respective officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses, including reasonable attorneys’ fees and court costs, that arise out of or relate to any breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party or its Representatives. This obligation of confidentiality shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement and shall continue for as long as the Confidential Information remains a secret and has not entered the public domain through no fault of the Receiving Party.
9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, neither Party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, business opportunities, or data, arising out of or related to this Agreement, even if advised of the possibility of such damages. This limitation shall not apply to damages arising from a Party’s gross negligence, willful misconduct, fraud, or breach of confidentiality obligations.
10. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall come into effect on the date of execution by both Parties and shall remain in force until the completion of the Project, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms herein.
Either Party (“Terminating Party”) shall have the right to terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days’ prior written notice to the other Party (“Non Terminating Party”) in the event of any material breach of the terms or conditions set forth in this Agreement by the Non Terminating Party. Such termination shall be without prejudice to any other rights or remedies available to the Terminating Party under applicable law or equity.
Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, all Confidential Information shall be returned in accordance with Clause 5 of this Agreement, and the obligations of confidentiality shall continue to survive as specified in this Agreement.
14. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Agreement is found invalid or unenforceable, the remainder shall remain in full force. Any such provision shall be replaced with one that reflects the original intent and is valid.

The arbitration tribunal shall be composed of a sole arbitrator, and such arbitrator shall be appointed mutually by the Parties. The place of arbitration shall be Mumbai, Maharashtra and the arbitration proceedings shall take place in the English language.
